# Getting familiar with Employees DB

Type: Exercise

## Course summary

In this part we are going to focus on revising everything you have learned in the course. Because we already know `Sakila DB` quite well, we are going to change the exercise database to `Employees DB`.

It consists of only 6 tables but a huge number of rows (millions) In the form of a discussion, together with the lecturer we will analyze the logical representation of the `employees` base:

## Questions:

1. What are the primary keys in the individual tables?
2. What relationships do particular pairs of tables have?
3. What foreign keys are there between the individual pairs of tables?
4. In the `salaries` table, how can you infer that a record is current?
