# Natural and migratory movement

Type: Exercise

In the file **Population in Poland - projections.xlsx** you will find a sheet **natural and migratory movement**.

Within a single figure, create two plots **closely together (next to each o other horizontally)**:

- births and deaths (two lines, based on the columns **Natural increase - Births** and **Natural decrease - Deaths** in the sheet)
- emigration and immigration (two lines, based on the columns **Foreign permanent migration - Immigration** and **Foreign permanent migration - Emigration** in the sheet)

Add legends to each graph explaining the meaning of each line.
