# Wrocław participatory budget

Type: Exercise

In the file **wpb_votes_list_2019.csv** you will find data about the votes cast within the Wroclaw Citizens' Budget. Data was downloaded from [Wrocław Open Data](https://www.wroclaw.pl/open-data/dataset/wroclawski-budzet-obywatelski-glosowanie).

Read this data and display a histogram of voters' ages - display bars covering 5-year ranges: voters aged 18 to 23, 24 to 28, etc. (up to 100 years - we assume this is the maximum age of voters).
