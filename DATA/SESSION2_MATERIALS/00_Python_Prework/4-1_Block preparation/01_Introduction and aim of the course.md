# Introduction and aim of the course

Type: Article

## Welcome to the Python - Data Analysis course

We want to welcome you warmly to the course that focuses primarily on the Python programming language and its applications in data analysis.

What to expect? During prework and early classes you will learn the basics of programming in the Python language. Next you are going to get the knowledge on methods of retrieving data from different sources: various file formats, databases, websites and APIs of programs and portals. Further on in the course you will see how to use the Pandas library to analyze data, generate charts and prepare reports. In the last class, together with the lecturer you will create a project that from day one is going to conduct data analysis of job offers in Poland. The project is going to be focused on combining all the skills acquired during the course.

But let's start at the beginning. This and subsequent articles will familiarize you with:

- the aim of the course
- the structure of the sessions and the topics covered,
- the structure and arrangement of materials
- the sequence of reading the articles and completing the exercises in prework
- the rules of the course

**You need to review the prepared materials carefully and thoroughly.**

> **Start working independently as soon as possible to better acquire the material that is new to you.** This will make your work most effective.

## The aim of the course

The goal of the **Python - Data Analysis** course is to provide a very solid and detailed introduction to the technologies used in data analysis. The range of technologies and libraries used by people involved in data analysis is wide, however, we have tried to prepare materials that will help you learn the most important solutions that support analysts.

We are confident that with our materials and with the help of the great lecturers you will meet at our school, learning is going to be fun and rewarding for you. It doesn't matter what your occupation has been: archaeology, trading or raising ostriches in Canada - the course starts from the basics.
