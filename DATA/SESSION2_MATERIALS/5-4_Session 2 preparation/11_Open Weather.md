# Open Weather

Type: Article

[At this address](https://openweathermap.org/current) you are going to find the documentation of the Open Weather API of the [openweathermap.org](https://openweathermap.org/) website. It describes endpoints that allow you to retrieve weather for a specific city, geographic coordinates or zip code. However, it does not return this data to everyone - only to those who send the correct key in the query.

To obtain the key, register at [openweathermap.org](https://openweathermap.org/) and confirm your email. After verification, another e-mail will provide your individual key, which you're going to use in all queries. You'll find your key also at [home.openweathermap.org/api_keys](https://home.openweathermap.org/api_keys).

**Task 1** will help you confirm that your key works.

> **Note:**
> 
> Keys are not active right away! In practice, it becomes active within 10 minutes to two hours**.
