# DataGrip license activation

Type: Article

After the first session your mentor is going to email you the license key for `JetBrains` necessary to activate `DataGrip`.

## Steps

### Checking inbox

After you receive your mentor's confirmation that keys were sent. go to your inbox and find the email sent from `no-reply@coderslab.pl`.

> If you don't see the message, check your `spam` folder.

### Key registration at `JetBrains`

The next step is to go to the following page:

[https://www.jetbrains.com/store/redeem/](https://www.jetbrains.com/store/redeem/)

Fill out all the fields as requested, and in the `Tools & IDEs` section choose `DataGrip`:

![email](/presentations/DTL/en/4.9/W/M_02_S_01/b000d210-04ad-428c-97c2-8c1b1f4ac13c/student_content/6abdeb55-6f25-4132-99bb-0b26dcb3dbdc/./images/02_selection.PNG)

> Make sure that `DataGrip` is chosen - there is no way to change it after the activation!

The completed page should look similar to this:

![email](/presentations/DTL/en/4.9/W/M_02_S_01/b000d210-04ad-428c-97c2-8c1b1f4ac13c/student_content/6abdeb55-6f25-4132-99bb-0b26dcb3dbdc/./images/03_form_filled_en.PNG)

After activation, the following message should appear:

![email](/presentations/DTL/en/4.9/W/M_02_S_01/b000d210-04ad-428c-97c2-8c1b1f4ac13c/student_content/6abdeb55-6f25-4132-99bb-0b26dcb3dbdc/./images/04_success.PNG)

### Activation in `DataGrip`

To complete the activation run `DataGrip` and, unless the activation window has opened, choose:

 
To complete the activation, go back to your email inbox, you should find an email there with the following title:

**License Certificate for JetBrains DataGrip / Order XXXXXXX**

In the email you will find the following link:

![email](./images/05_jetbains_email.png)

When you click on the link, a page with license provisions will be displayed - you should read them and then (if you want) accept them.

Once it is accepted you'll be taken to the `JetBrains` website where our license ID can be found:
!

![email](/presentations/DTL/en/4.9/W/M_02_S_01/b000d210-04ad-428c-97c2-8c1b1f4ac13c/student_content/6abdeb55-6f25-4132-99bb-0b26dcb3dbdc/./images/06_login_page.png)

And follow the forms displayed on the page.

> When logging in, use the same email address that the license was sent to.

After logging in, we click the `Active` -> `Activate new licence` buttons.

### Process completed

## Notes

- You have **two weeks** after you receive the license to activate it - after this time the license is canceled,
- if a `JetBrains` license had been sent to the email address before, activating it again is not going to be possible: in that case contact your mentor to solve the problem,
- The license is valid for six months from the date you receive it,
- The license is educational, i.e. **can be used on business equipment, but not commercially**.

If you have further questions or problems, please contact your mentor.
