# Introduction

Type: Article

Welcome to the course fully dedicated to the `SQL` language and its applications in data analysis. During the prework you will learn the basics of working with a database, and you will get the explanations of the basic concepts of creating queries. Then you'll learn about data processing, types of joins, and finally about data aggregation methods and creating your own functions on the database.

This article will familiarize you with:

- aim of the course,
- structure and topics of sessions
- rules of the course

**You need to review the prepared materials carefully and thoroughly.**

**Start working on your own as soon as possible to better acquire the material that is new to you.** This will allow you to work efficiently during the course.

# The aim of the course

The `SQL - data analysis` course aims to provide an in-depth introduction to this technology in data analytics. The scope of the material has been selected in such a way as to allow you to become comprehensively familiar with the tools needed in the daily work of an analyst.

We are confident that thanks to the materials created on the basis of many years of experience in data analysis using `SQL`, after the course you will have no problems meeting the demands of an analyst's daily life.
