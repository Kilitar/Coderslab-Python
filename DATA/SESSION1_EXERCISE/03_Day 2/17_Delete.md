## Delete

The table `tasks.films_to_be_cleaned` is a copy of the `film` table. We want to remove the movies that meet the following criteria:

- `category_id` in (1, 5, 7, 9),
- `length` is less than 1 hour,
- `rating` is not `NC-17` or `PG`.

Use `JOIN` to complete the exercise. After executing your query, write another one to check its correctness.
