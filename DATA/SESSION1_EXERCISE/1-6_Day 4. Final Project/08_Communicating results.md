# Communicating results

Type: Exercise

1. Open the `ufc_fights.xlsx` file, with the results of the previous exercise.
2. Based on the results obtained earlier in the data exploration steps, prepare visualizations (if necessary).
3. Put the visualizations and numerical information into a PowerPoint presentation. Remember about:

  3. selecting appropriate types of charts and making sure they are readable.
4. Save the results of your work in Excel in the `ufc_fights.xlsx` and in PowerPoint (choose your own name for the file).
