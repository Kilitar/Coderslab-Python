# Exploring data

Type: Exercise

1. Open the `ufc_fights.xlsx` file, with the results of the previous exercise.
2. Explore the data:
  
  1. for numerical variables, calculate the average, median, minimum value, maximum value, standard deviation, and 1st and 3rd quartile,
  2. for categorical variables, calculate the number of unique values,
  3. for dates, determine the earliest and newest date.
3. Using pivot tables, group the data to get the answers to the questions asked during the presentation, as well as the questions you formulated during the problem definition task. Save the results in separate tables so that you can use them in further stages of the analysis.
4. Save the results of your work.
