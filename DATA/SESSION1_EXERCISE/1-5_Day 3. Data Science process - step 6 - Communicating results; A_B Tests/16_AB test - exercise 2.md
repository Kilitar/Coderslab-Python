# AB test - exercise 2

Type: Exercise

In the free version of Spotify, for every 15 minutes of listening to a user, a banner ad is shown on the app screen for 15 seconds. We have a hypothesis that reducing the time will increase the number of users with at least one ad clicked.

1. Download and open the file with AB test data: `ab_test_exercises.xlsx`.
2. In the `ab_test_2` tab you'll find the results of the A/B, that:
  
  
  
  1. in the **A** version of the app, we tested a mechanism that showed a banner ad for **15 seconds**, and in the **B** version for **10 seconds**,
  2. the purpose of the test is to determine whether users from version B are more likely to click the ads than the users assigned to version A,
  3. lasted 3 days,
  4. level of statistical significance was set at **0.05**.
3. Perform an aggregation of the test results and record the results in a separate table.
4. Using the [AB TestGuide](https://abtestguide.com/calc/), calculator decide whether the test is statistically significant and whether it yielded an increase. If there is no statistical significance, consider what could be done to make the test achieve it.

### Good luck!
