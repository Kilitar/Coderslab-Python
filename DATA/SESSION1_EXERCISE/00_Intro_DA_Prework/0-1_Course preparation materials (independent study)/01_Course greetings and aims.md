# Course greetings and aims

Type: Article

## What are you going to do?

Welcome to the course: an introduction to data analysis and other related topics. In this course, we do not require knowledge of any programming language. This course focuses on a good understanding of Microsoft Excel 365 and its application in data analysis. During *prework* you are going to learn the basics of:

- data analysis process,
- popular methodologies,
- organizing and processing data, statistics and samples of data visualization

In addition, you'll install MS Excel 365 and learn its functionalities. We will also introduce you to the community of data analysts clustered around the Kaggle platform and its open datasets.

The knowledge gained during the *prework* will let you efficiently participate in the classes during the two two-day sessions. In the sessions, we'll guide you through the complex process of data analysis. At the same time, we'll introduce you to the Excel 365 functionalities that have been created to implement this process. They are:

- functions and formulas,
- Advanced data filters,
- joining methods,
- aggregation,
- visualizing data on various types of charts.

## Final project

In the **"Introduction to Data Analysis "** course, you'll find more than data analysis tools. We will teach you how to discover and tell the hidden stories in data (*story telling*) in a clear and accessible way. In the last class, you are going to create a project that will involve conducting data analysis from start to finish. The project will be a test for you of all the skills you are going to learn in the course.

## The aim of the course

The purpose of the **Introduction to Data Analysis** course is to give you a **solid introduction to the process of data analysis** without having to learn any programming languages. This course is designed for people who want to learn about data analysis and significantly improve their Excel skills. Despite the presence of a large amount of advanced technology in data analysis, Excel is still widely used - as a tool in the initial stages of working with new data sets. This is due to its flexibility and intuitiveness of use.

We are confident that with our materials and working with our lecturers, with whom you'll interact during the course, you're going to enjoy learning and stimulate your curiosity. Regardless of what you've done before: music, dance, or catering, the course starts from absolute basics. We also believe that you'll be able to use the knowledge and skills you acquired here, not only in your work, but also in everyday life.
