# The Joy of Stats - inspiration

Type: Article

It is said that watching the best is a good way to learn. For this reason, we'll start with a great example of **story telling** using data, which we will study later in the course.
We invite you to watch an inspiring excerpt from *The Joy of Stats*, where we will learn the story of how quality of life has changed in 200 countries over the last 200 years in just... 4 minutes.

The *The Joy of Stats* program was produced by BBC in cooperation with [Hans Rosling](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hans_Rosling), a professor of international health at the Swedish Karolinska Institute and a well known proponent of fact-based education. Rosling co-authored the posthumously published the book *Factfulness*, a worldwide bestseller, in which he introduces the general public to how statistics and data analysis can give us a different perspective on the world around us.
