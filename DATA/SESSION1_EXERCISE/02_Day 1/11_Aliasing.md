# Aliasing

Type: Exercise

## Aliasing

1. Display the data from `sakila.rental` giving aliases to columns according to the requirements:
  
  
  
  - leave the columns `rental_id`, `inventory_id`, `customer_id` unchanged,
  - change `rental_date` column to `date_of_rental`,
  - change `return_date` column to `date_of_rental_return`,
  - list the columns in the order of your choice.
2. Display the data from `sakila.rental` and translate names of the following column from English to your language:
  
  
  
  - `rental_id`
  - `inventory_id`
  - `rental_date`
  - `return_date`
