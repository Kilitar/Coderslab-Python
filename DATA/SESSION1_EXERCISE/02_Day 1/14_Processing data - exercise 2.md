# Processing data - exercise 2

Type: Exercise

1. Based on the `energy` variable create an `energy_category` variable, that defines the energy level, whether the song mostly consists of words, words and music, or mainly music.
  
  
  
  
  For `energy`:
  
  
  
  - above 0.66 assign the `High` value,
  - greater than 0.33 and less or equal 0.66 assign the `Medium` value,
  - less than or equal 0.33 assign the `Low` value
2. Based on the`valence` variable, determine the emotional characteristic of the song (positive or negative)
  
  
  
  
  For `valence`:
  
  
  
  - greater or equal 0.5, assign the `Positive` value,
  - less than 0.5, assign the `Negative` value.
  
  
  
  Save the result in the `song_mood` variable.

### Good luck!
