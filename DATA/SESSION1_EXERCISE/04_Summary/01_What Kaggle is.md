# What Kaggle is

Type: Article

During this *prework* the sample dataset came from the [Kaggle.com](https://www.kaggle.com/) platform.

## Kaggle — Booking.com for data people

Why is Kaggle the Booking.com for data analysts and data scientists? It's where many data enthusiasts spend their evenings and weekends. Kaggle is a popular online community for people closely involved in data analysis and machine learning.

The main goal of this platform (founded by Anthony Goldbloom and Jeremy Howard in 2010, and later acquired by Google in 2017) is to help professionals and beginners achieve their goals during their data analysis journey, thanks to the powerful tools and resources it provides.

Additionally, Kaggle provides a cloud-based environment. It makes it easy to get started with notebooks that use GPUs (*Graphics Processing Unit*). GPUs are used to solve complex machine learning problems.

## What can I do on Kaggle?

- download and share datasets
- browse notebooks (usually with code written in Python/R) with analyses from other users (as many as **8 million!** of them)
- discuss datasets and analyses with other users
- collect points, related to activity on the portal (e.g. giving helpful tips to other users)
- take part in competitions organized by companies to solve business problems using machine learning techniques
- build your repository with analyses to show off at a job interview (Kaggle is a kind of **"GitHub for data analysts "**).

## Kaggle competitions

Kaggle allows data analysts (and other developers) to participate in machine learning competitions. The types of data analysis problems can involve literally anything, from trying to predict the occurrence of cancer by studying patient records to analyzing the sentiment of movie reviews to discover their impact on audience reaction.

Projects on Kaggle are published by various entities. While some are just for educational purposes, others are real problems that companies are trying to solve. The rewards for solving a problem are not only in the form of money, but can also include attractive prizes such as a job offer or free products from the company that organizes the contest.

For most users involved in the platform, cash prizes are the most exciting - Home Depot, for example, offered a prize of $40000 to the winner of a search engine optimization algorithm for homedepot.com.

### Do the Kaggle contest projects have a real impact on the world?

Sure! One of the most recognizable projects is `Heritage Health`, that offered the prize of as much as 3 million dollars. The competition was based on finding solutions to facilitate HIV/AIDS diagnostics. Several scientific articles have been written based on the resulting projects.

It's safe to say that Kaggle has given companies the opportunity to solve their problems while involving the best data specialists in the world.

### Datasets

From our perspective, that is, at the beginning of the journey in the world of data analysis, the most useful component is datasets. Kaggle allows you to filter sets by defined parameters, as well as search by title.

![Datasets](/presentations/DTL/en/4.9/W/M_00_S_05/80de05a3-e70d-47dd-b26d-1ca169e244ba/student_content/a0785332-3ef4-4454-8259-c9b8c233cbfd/images/Kaggle_3.png)

After entering the page with the selected dataset, we can see a detailed description of the data. In addition, we know under what license, the collection was published, and we also know its usability.
The value of this parameter is calculated by Kaggle and determines how good the documentation associated with our dataset is.
To start working with a dataset, we can either create a notebook directly in Kaggle or download the source file and load it into any tool.
At this stage we're forced to choose the second option, due to the fact that we will be doing the analysis in Excel.

## Summary

Kaggle is a very interesting platform that brings together a huge number of data specialists from all over the world.
Thanks to the large number of data sets, you can constantly develop your analytical skills, which at some point may allow you to participate in Kaggle contests.
There, prizes and a lot of satisfaction await you as you solve problems in various fields.
